The Question

Examine the decision by [Business X] to launch a new product into an international market. [10 marks]

⚠ Weak Student Response — read carefully

"Business X is considering launching its new product internationally. A SWOT analysis shows that the business has many strengths, weaknesses, opportunities and threats. One strength is that the business has a strong brand. A weakness is that it has high costs. An opportunity is the growing international market and a threat is competition from rivals. The Ansoff Matrix is a tool used to identify growth strategies. Business X could use market development to enter the new market. In conclusion, Business X should launch internationally because the Ansoff Matrix and SWOT analysis both suggest this is a good idea."
